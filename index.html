About

Welcome to our charming flat in Barcelona available for holiday rental.

A light & airy flat at the heart of the medieval city. Arenes de sant pere is a peaceful, sunny haven above the rooftops, close to the main action – the ramblas, el born and the picasso museum - yet in a vibrant, happening neighbourhood that’s delightfully free of tourists.

Sleeps up to five people. One double bedroom, a double pull-out sofa-bed, and a further single bedroom upstairs.
